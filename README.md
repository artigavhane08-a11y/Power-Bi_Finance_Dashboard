# 📊 Finance Dashboard – Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview
This Finance Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze key financial metrics and business performance. It provides insights into sales, profit, gross sales, discounts, product performance, customer segments, and country-wise sales through interactive visualizations.

The dashboard is divided into two pages:
- **Home Dashboard** – High-level business overview with KPIs and summary charts.
- **Detailed Analysis** – In-depth analysis using advanced visuals and filters.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor overall business performance.
- Compare sales and profit across different countries.
- Analyze product-wise profitability.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Understand customer segment contribution.
- Enable interactive filtering and page navigation.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Home Dashboard
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Units Sold KPI
- Total Gross Sales KPI
- Sales by Country
- Profit by Product
- Sales by Segment
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Interactive slicers

### Detailed Analysis
- Sales by Country and Segment
- Profit by Country
- Sales by Product
- Monthly Sales vs Profit Trend
- Sales Distribution by Country (Treemap)
- Interactive slicers
- Navigation buttons between pages
